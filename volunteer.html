Internship and Practicum fro Students

As Help Change My City Alliance we do everything we can to support and empower new graduates and current students to advance personal growth and career development through volunteering and making a positive contribution to our community. We look to give business work experience to current students and graduates and provide references for their chosen career field.

If you are interested on interning with us please e-mail a copy of your resume to alpha@helpchangemycity.ca
